Resources

Here is a list of helpful resources for research and scholarship that I will do my best to keep updated.

Useful References

  • Seven Research Contributions in HCI. -- A resource for identifying and clarifying the types of research contributions in HCI.
    Source: Jacob O. Wobbrock. 2012.

  • Catchy Titles Are Good: But Avoid Being Cute. -- A resource for writing and structuring HCI research papers.
    Source: Jacob O. Wobbrock. 2015.

  • Choosing the right Statistical Test in SAS, STATA, SPSS and R. -- A guide resource for choosing appropriate statistical tests.
    Source: IDRE, UCLA.

  • Resources for running a research group. -- A repository of resources for running and managing a research group.
    Source: Jean Yang.

  • Reviewing a Manuscript for Publication. -- A guide to reviewing manuscripts.
    Source: Alan Lee. 1995.

  • SIGACCESS Resources. -- A collection of community resources including accessible writing and presentation guides.
    Source: ACM SIGACCESS.

  • Paragraphs. -- A resource for constructing and writing paragraphs.
    Source: The Writing Center at UNC .
